Mission


The intent of this organization is to support student participation in the field of Linguistics and the Teaching of English for Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) by providing broader access to resources at both the academic and professional levels. Recently we have resumed the publication of original Working Papers written by students in the Linguistis and Writing and Rhetoric programs through our student-run journal - Working Papers in Language and Linguistics (WPILL). You can find this by clicking the "Publications" tab above.


The club will also promote monthly or bi-monthly events - activities ranging from linguistically relevant films to sponsored speakers and game nights.


Membership is free. For membership information please contact Sylvia Schreiner.
